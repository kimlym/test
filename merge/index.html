About Us

The Massage Center is open for business on Monday through Saturday between 8 AM and 7 PM. We have many skilled licensed massage therapists available to provide relaxation, rejuvenation and restoration of balance to your body.

We are located in a very convenient location with 24 hr parking. It is easily accessible and situated in a clean and safe locality. The surroundings are very peaceful and quiet to help you relax during the massage.

Most of our therapists have atleast 5 to 8 years experience and a degree in the field. Several of us hold an additional Master Bodyworker certification. We are all easygoing, friendly and focused on the healing aspects of our profession.

What We Do

Body Massage

Otherwise known as classic massage used to support the long-term health and rehabilitative care of individuals and athletes. Firm but gentle pressure is used with long flowing movements for total relaxation. Increased circulation and relief of muscle tension are results of this massage.

Stone Massage

Hot stone massage is a specialty massage where the therapist uses smooth, heated stones, by placing them on the body. The heat can be both deeply relaxing and help warm up tight muscles so the therapist can work more deeply, more quickly.

Foot Massage

The genuine reflexology is the application of pressure onto particular areas of the soles of the feet. A reflex action in another part of the body is stimulated by the manipulation of each specific area.

Head Massage

A treatment based on old Ayurvedic techniques, involving work on the upper back, shoulders, neck, scalp and face. It helps reduce stress, fatigue, and eyestrain, increase mental clarity, relaxes and rejuvenates the receiver
